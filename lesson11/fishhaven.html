What's Fish Haven?

Fish Haven is one of Idaho's many hidden secrets. It is a small town North East of Idaho Falls. In align with its name, Fish Haven is exactly what you would expect... a fisherman's paradise! Snuggled up along the Snake River, Fish Haven has many fishing opportunities. It is alos very popular for those who enjoy recreational activities such as skiing, hiking, camping, motorsports, and hunting!
You wont find a hotel to stay at due to the small population of 340 residents. Make sure to bring a tent or a trailer! Fish Haven is known for having a friendly, close knit community.
