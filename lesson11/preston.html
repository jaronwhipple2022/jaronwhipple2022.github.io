What's Biting?

The fishing near Preston Idaho has been incredible! As reported by local fisherman, there has been an incresingly high amount of the commonly caught rainbow trout. There are also larger amounts of brown trout reported. These are more sought after when compared to rainbow trout because they are often a lot bigger in size. What fisherman doesn't enjoy landing a large fish? Fly fishing is the most commonly used method, as there are lots of streams nearby. Many Preston locals also enjoy traveling a few hours to access larger water sources such as the Snake River, or Bear lake.
